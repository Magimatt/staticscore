Staticscore Movie Rankings

Posiscores are measure of a movie's quality. Objectively, did the movie have quality acting, writing, cinematography, practical effects? This score doesn't take into account how much you liked the movie, so don't let your personal enjoyment influence this score.

Negascores are the opposite of posiscores. They are all about subjectivity. Who cares how incredible (or utterly awful) the CGI was! Negascores measures how much you like a movie. Let this score show how beloved or despised you find the number one critic's choice (or how you unironically adore "The Room").
